Omerta | Asian Fusion

"A modern culinary experience in Dhaka."

Omerta is a contemporary Asian fusion restaurant located in the heart of Dhaka. We offer a unique dining experience that combines traditional Asian flavors with modern culinary techniques. Our menu features a diverse range of dishes inspired by the rich culinary heritage of Asia, including Chinese, Japanese, Thai, and Korean cuisines.

At Omerta, we are committed to using only the freshest ingredients and authentic spices to create bold and flavorful dishes. Our chefs are passionate about their craft and strive to deliver an unforgettable dining experience for our guests. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner, a family gathering, or a casual meal with friends, Omerta is the perfect destination for food lovers in Dhaka.
